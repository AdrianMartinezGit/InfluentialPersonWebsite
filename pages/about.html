WHO IS "NOTCH"?

Persson was born in Stockholm, Sweden, during his early life, his childhood seemed a bit rough as his parents had eventually gone through a divorce, living with his mother along with his sister. Markus had gained an interest in video games at an early game, having a Commodore 128 to play bootleg games, and load in programs from the help of computer magazines, learning how to program games at the age of 7, making his own text-based adventure game. Markus had a vision of becoming a video game developer, spending his spare time with games and programming at home, reverse engineering the Quake-based DOOM engine.
