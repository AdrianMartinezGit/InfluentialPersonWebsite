Why has ‘‘Notch’’ has been influential to me?

Ever since I was young and had an imagination of my own, which was a simpler time as a child, Minecraft was a way for me to express my creativity in building structures and meeting new friends who had the same interests as I did. As I grew older and started to dive into the field of programming, I would look back at Minecraft and how it amazes me with its procedural generation and other technical aspects that I’ve wanted to become a programmer and eventually a video game developer.
